Quality

Every Simon G. piece goes through between 8-10 quality control inspections.

  • Each production process step is handled by one craftsman who specializes in that particular job.
  • Every step in the process is managed by a perfectionist.
  • Each Simon C. piece is assigned a unique serial number that is inscribed on the piece.
  • Each piece is delivered with an Authenticity Card that communicates to your client that they are buying a piece of Simon G. Jewelry that is guaranteed authentic and covered under the Simon G. manufacturer’s warranty.

Minimum standards to deliver maximum brilliance and shine:

For Bridal:

  • VS clarity
  • F-G color
  • Very good - excellent cut

White, pink, and yellow diamonds are naturally colored

  • All diamonds are conflict-free
  • All diamonds and gemstones are hand-set with 4 prongs on every stone.
  • Patented Simon Set pieces allow for the look of invisible set jewelry, with the strength of prong setting.
  • Each setting is inspected under a microscope by seasoned artisans.
  • Ensuring that all diamonds are securely set offers you and your client peace of mind.

Platinum

Simon G. platinum is alloyed with iridium, which prevents the development of a gun-metal color patina appearing over time.

This alloy process helps to form the strongest platinum for cast jewelry. This allows for the creation of the intricate designs Simon G. is recognized for.

A Word About Platinum

When asked, over 74% of brides today say they would prefer platinum for their engagement ring.

Many don't get what they want because their sales consultant fails to demonstrate of value of platinum.

REMEMBER: Value is not about how much the client spends. It is about what they GET for what they spend!

18K Gold

Simon G. 18K white gold is alloyed with palladium to ensure that the jewelry retains a crisp white color, and is stronger than the market standard. and remains hypoallergenic.

  • Extremely low nickel content in the alloy.
  • Metal is more durable than standard white gold, retains a brilliant white color and will not need rhodium plating as often.
  • Every side of each piece is carefully finished and polished.
  • Each piece is beautiful from the bottom and sides as well as from the top.
  • Quality shows from every angle.

Bridal Ring Size Pricing Policy

Simon G. Jewelry is pleased to announce two new policies that will significantly improve your ability to estimate retail pricing at the point of sale with our bridal jewelry.

Effective February 1, 2016, our change fee structure will change to the following:

Bridal Ring Size:

  • All Bridal Rings (ER and Bands) will remain the same price up to size 9
  • Ring size 9.25-11 will incur an additional charge of $110.00 MSRP
  • Ring size 11.25-12 will incur an additional charge of $220.00 MSRP

Head Size:

Engagement rings and sets

There will be no additional charge when changing center stone size between .75ct - 2.00ct for same shape diamond center stones

  • Princess and round center stones only
  • Piece must be a current item
  • Some rings subject to additional charge
    ie: extensive modification requests, fancy shaped side stones present

Policy changes only available on special orders, not available for stock orders.
